![](/images/image-18.png)

Swap Center is a convenient way to trade with outstanding speed. This window appears on any token page.

### Swap Center allows you to specify:

* Custom slippage & tip for **buy transactions**
* Exact buy amount
* Percentage to sell

<Danger>
**Buy Amount** and **Sell Percentage** are **action buttons**—clicking them will instantly execute a buy or sell transaction. You only need to use the **Buy** and **Sell** buttons if you want to enter **custom values** before executing the trade.
</Danger>

***
