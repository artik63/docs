title: "Setup"
---

In order to connect the extension with the bot you need to open the bot on the same machine (PC / VPS) as the extension. Additionally, you can look on [remote-access-secure.md](/addons/blood-qt-extension/tips/remote-access-secure) or [remote-access-insecure.md](/addons/blood-qt-extension/tips/remote-access-insecure) to be able to run extension and bot on separate machines.

* For positions management you don't need to start any tasks, just open the bot and you are good to go!
* To be able to perform swaps you need to:\
  \- Open the extension in **`new tab`** or in **`side panel`** using buttons on the [homepage.md](/addons/blood-qt-extension/tabs/homepage) or [settings.md](/addons/blood-qt-extension/tabs/settings)\
  \- Open the bot\
  \- Create worker group (task file) with watch mode and start it!

Here you can find default task, feel free to change and adapt it to your needs.
