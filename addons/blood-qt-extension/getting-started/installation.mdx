---
title: "Installation"
---

Only install extension using our discord slash command.

1. Download any browser that has chrome extensions support (Chrome, Firefox, Arc).
2. Head over to our **Discord.**
3. Open **`#commands`** channel and use **`/qt-extension-download-url`** slash command. After executing the command, click on the link and download the extension.
4. Extract contents of the extension.
5. Open your browser, click on the three dots in the top-right corner (⋮) and navigate to: **`Settings > Extensions`** Or, directly type **`chrome://extensions`** into the address bar and hit **Enter.**

6. At the top right of the **`Extensions`** page, **toggle** **on** **`Developer mode`**.
7. Click the **`Load unpacked`** button Navigate to the folder containing your extracted extension files and select it.\
   Browser will load the extension, and you should see it listed on the **Extensions** page.

<Warning>
You have to load folder which DIRECTLY contains **manifest.json** file, otherwise you won't be able to load it.
</Warning>

8. Verify that the extension appears in the Chrome toolbar If it doesn’t show by default, click on the puzzle piece icon (Extensions menu) in the top-right corner.\
   Pin your extension for easier access.
