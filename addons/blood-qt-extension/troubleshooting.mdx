### The message port closed before a response was received.

* _**Solution**_ - open extension in a new tab OR in side panel.

<Note>
You need to open ONLY ONE window. Opening multiple extension tabs will ruin positions list and you won't be able to sell position on the page.
</Note>

### Swap failed: Failed to fetch

* _**Solution**_ - recheck your connection link as extension can't connect to bot.

### Swap failed: \[2] no swap subscribers available

* _**Solution**_ - start task with watch mode on your instance with bot.
