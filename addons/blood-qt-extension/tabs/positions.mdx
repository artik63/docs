---
title: "Positions"
---

Positions page is used to easily manage positions that are stored in your **`BLOOD`**

In the initial setup, you won't see any positions, so the page will look like this

<Frame caption="Positions (no positions)">
  <img src="/images/Screenshot-2024-12-08-at-16.45.18.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

However, you can see your active and stored positions by click on Memory and DB checkmarks and then pressing Refresh button

<Frame caption="Positions">
  <img src="/images/Screenshot-2024-12-08-at-16.52.59.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

You can manage your positions by right clicking on the desired position or you can get a full overview of the position and controls by just clicking on it with the left click\
Moreover, you can search and filter your positions based on your needs\
Position statuses are automatically updated every 2 seconds, so there is no need to click the “Refresh” button.
