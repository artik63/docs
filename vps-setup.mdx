### VPS Requirements

<Tip>
Min Hardware: **2 CPU/4 RAM**\
Recommended Hardware: **8 CPU/12 RAM**\
_**High clock speed CPU**_ will be a great choice

Location: **New-York/Frankfurt/Amsterdam** OR **same as your RPC/Geyser provider**
</Tip>

### Protection

After receiving server IP and password make sure to do following to secure it:

<Steps>
<Step title="Generate a new password">
Creating complicated and long password is a crucial step to make hackers life harder

We recommend to generate **AT LEAST 32 SYMBOL PASSWORD** including uppercase/lowercase/numbers/special symbols. 

Don't forget it to save on your local machine in TXT or put it on paper

<Note>
You can use special websites for generating password. 

Example: [https://www.lastpass.com/features/password-generator](https://www.lastpass.com/features/password-generator)
</Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Password change">
Now head to the `Settings → Accounts → Sign-in options → Password change`

![](/images/image-8.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Download Evil Watcher">
**Evil Watcher** is a security tool designed to monitor and protect your system from suspicious activity in real time. It watches for changes in files, processes, and registry keys, helping to detect malware, unauthorized modifications, or potential security threats

[Download Link](https://github.com/devnulli/EvlWatcher?tab=readme-ov-file)
</Step>
</Steps>
