### Bot stopped working and has "Connected :x:" in topbar

Windows Defender deleted CORE.EXE in working directory. To avoid it, 

* open `Settings` and search `windows security`
* Click on `Security at a glance`

![](/images/image-33.png)

* Click `Virus & threat protection`. Head to `Manage settings`

![](/images/image-34.png)

* Click `Add or remove exclusions`

![](/images/image-36.png)

* Put `C:\Users\Administrator` into path, click on `bloodv2` and press `Select folder`. Enjoy!

![](/images/image-37.png)
